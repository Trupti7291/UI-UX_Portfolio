Myflix

Watch Unlimited Video Streaming.

Myflix is an online streaming and rental service that offers a wide selection of entertainment options, including popular films, television series, web series, and TV shows to its users. In addition to streaming content, it also allows users to rent or purchase videos.

Myflix offers a comprehensive library of entertainment options that users can access from a variety of devices, including mobile phones, smart TVs, computers, and tablets. This makes it easy for users to watch their favorite films, TV shows, and web series from wherever they are, as long as they have an internet connection. With so many different types of content available in one place, Myflix offers a convenient and enjoyable way for users to stay entertained.

    Discover

    A design focusing on Myflix app.

    Instead of diving headfirst into designing the app, I took the discovery route to address the core challenges of why Myflix was created in the first place and what would motivate users to use the platform. Understanding the thoughts and emotions regarding Myflix is an essential step in ensuring that the platform will be successful and that users will want to use it.

    By following a process to discover and understand user needs and motivations, I can ensure that Myflix app will meet their needs and provide a valuable experience. This process can involve conducting user research, analyzing market trends, and identifying user pain points and goals.

    Taking this approach allows to validate if the project is something of interest to users, which can save the time and resources in the long run. It also helps to create a user-centered design that prioritizes the needs and preferences of the target audience.

    Competitive Analysis

    Conducting a competitive analysis on existing mobile video streaming apps is an excellent approach to understand the strengths and weaknesses of similar platforms in the market. By analyzing apps like YouTube and Prime Videos, I can identify their pain points and opportunities for improvement, which can help inform the design and development of my own app.

    Some areas to focus on during competitive analysis include:

    • User experience: How easy is it for users to find and access content? Is the interface intuitive and user-friendly?
    • Content library: What types of content are available? Are there any gaps or opportunities for differentiation?
    • Pricing and payment options: How do users pay for content? Are there any subscription options available?
    • Features and functionality: What unique features do these apps offer? Are there any opportunities for innovation or improvement?

    By identifying the strengths and weaknesses of existing apps, I can find opportunities to differentiate Myflix app and provide a better experience for users.

    User Research

    Collecting quantitative data is an important step in the discovery phase of app development as it provides with concrete insights into the preferences and behaviors of the target users. To start, I need to identify target audience and their approaches to using the app. Once having a clear understanding of target audience, I used tool like Google Survey, to ask them questions about their preferences and behaviors. This can include questions

    • What types of content they prefer?
    • How often they use video streaming apps?
    • What features they find most valuable?

    User stories

    Based on the survey responses and competitive analysis, I created a user story for both new and returning users to highlight their high-priority needs.

    As a new user
    • I want to be able to easily discover and access a wide variety of high-quality movies and TV shows from different genres and countries, so that I can find content that interests me and start watching right away.

    • I also want the app to provide personalized recommendations based on my viewing history and preferences, so that I can discover new content that I might enjoy.

    • In addition, I want the app to have a user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation, so that I can easily find the content I want and customize my viewing experience.

    • Finally, I want the app to offer flexible pricing options and a seamless payment process, so that I can choose a plan that fits my budget and easily subscribe or unsubscribe as needed.
    As a returning user
    • I want the app to continue providing me with a seamless and personalized viewing experience, so that I can easily pick up where I left off and discover new content that I might enjoy.

    • I also want the app to offer regular updates and new releases, so that I can stay up-to-date with the latest movies and TV shows.

    Design Documentation

    Formulated all design aspects on typography, color palettes, layout, and iconography that ensure consistency across all visual elements of the app.

    color palette

    Using the established branding of the Myflix logo and colour theme as a starting point is a great approach to ensure visual consistency across the brand. Incorporating complementary colors such as black, dark grey, light grey, medium grey, and secondary color can help to create a visually pleasing and cohesive design. Also accent colors helps to create a more visually engaging and effective user experience, as long as they are used thoughtfully and in a way that complements the overall design.

    Typography Styles

    Lora and Inter are great font choices for the Myflix app, as they are both highly readable and versatile typefaces that work well for both title and body text. Lora has a classic and elegant feel, which makes it a great choice for headlines and titles. Inter, on the other hand, is highly legible and has a neutral design that makes it versatile for use in both labels and body text. Inter's balanced and consistent letterforms also make it a great choice for digital interfaces, as it is easy to read on screens of different sizes and resolutions. The combination of Lora and Inter can help create a cohesive and polished visual identity for the Myflix app, while also ensuring that the app is easy to read and navigate for users.

    Iconography

    Iconography is an important aspect of any app's design, as it can help users quickly identify and understand different functions and features and and accomplish their desired tasks with ease. Myflix icons are simple, recognizable, and literal that can help users quickly understand the function or action associated with them.

    Enlightenment

    Reflecting on the UX design journey for Myflix in terms of what I've learned, what I can improve on, and why I started it.

    What I learned

    I have learned so much through the construction of Myflix, from implementing Material Design Guidelines to collaborating with others to further improve Myflix. Letting go of designs and ideas that are not serving usability purposes was difficult but essential for creating a better user experience. I, being open-minded and willing to iterate based on user feedback was crucial for creating a user-centered design. I've gained valuable experience and insights throughout this project Also learned that empathy is not a one-time action, but a continuous effort to understand and meet the needs of the users.

    I sought, received, and listen to feedback and critique from others, but equally important to have confidence in my own design choices and decisions. It's all about finding the right balance between being open to suggestions and feedback, while also standing steadfast to my design vision and objectives. This is a skill I can be honed through practice and experience, and it can ultimately lead to more potent and more effective design solutions.
