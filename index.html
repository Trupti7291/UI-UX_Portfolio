About

I am a certified UI/UX designer by Udemy with a web developer background holding a bachelor degree in Computer Applications with experince in languages such as HTML, CSS, C, C++, VB.net, DBMS. I worked as a receptionist in a private Hospital (MMPJ Hospital, Bhuj, India) for 6 Months and later as a Computer Operator, which involved working with MS Office and Software to manage patients reports.
